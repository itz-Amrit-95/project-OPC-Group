Smart World The Edition - Project Info

Situated in Golf Course Extension, Gurgaon, Smart World The Edition offers a luxurious and well-designed apartment living experience. The project features 961 units of 3 BHK - 4 BHK apartments spanning across 10.7 acres of land. The apartments range from 2945 sqft to 3505 sqft, providing ample space and comfort. With its three high-rise twin towers, convenient location near Golf Course Extension Road, and close proximity to Spaze Business Park and Emaar The Palm Square, Smart World The Edition ensures a well-connected and vibrant lifestyle. The project is also well connected to the Sector 55-56 Metro Station, providing easy access to the rest of the city.
