Hiranandani Group Brings A Pre-Diwali Extravaganza - Hiranandani Pulse

It’s time to save the dates 15th and 16th of October 2022!

Hiranandani Pulse is bringing to you a festival of music, dance, art, games, shopping, food, and much more. An experience so good and worth reminiscing about. We have for you an event that will make your weekend full of joy!

The Hiranandani Group is determined in creating a community that cares, loves, and spreads peace among each other. We believe in spreading happiness and making it more significant for everyone. This Diwali, we will make the whole experience even more fun, vibrant, and embellished with amusing entertainment at the Hiranandani Gardens, Powai (near castle rock).

An ornate presentation of Diwali decor will greet you to experience a captivating Pre-Diwali extravaganza that will host a Flea market, and Kids zone, paired with live performances The festival will be decorated with Diwali decor in the majestic Hiranandani Gardens, Powai. The event will also host a freestyle act from the BMX bikers.

Want to know what you don’t want to miss?

Let’s dive right in!

Is Shopping Your Forte?


Your happy hour of shopping is here!

Walk in on the 15th and 16th of October from 4 pm to 10 pm into the flea market. Shop for Diwali decor beforehand and find your favourite decor materials and many others at the flea market. Your shopping spree has found a place to be explored!
